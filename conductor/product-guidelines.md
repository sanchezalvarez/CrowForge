# Product Guidelines

## 1. Prose Style

All user-facing text in CrowForge should be written in a **friendly and casual** tone. The language should be approachable and encouraging, making the application feel like a helpful partner rather than a cold tool.

## 2. Branding

The brand identity of CrowForge is **creative and innovative**. This should be reflected in the application's design, marketing materials, and overall user experience. The brand should inspire users to think outside the box and explore new ideas.

## 3. UX Principles

The user experience of CrowForge should be guided by the following principles:

*   **Ease of Use:** The application must be intuitive and easy to learn, even for users who are not familiar with AI or other advanced software.
*   **Performance:** The application should be fast and responsive, providing a smooth and seamless user experience.
*   **Reliability:** The application must be reliable and error-free, ensuring that users can trust it with their important work.
