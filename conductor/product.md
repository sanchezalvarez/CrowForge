# Initial Concept

A local-first AI workspace with Chat, Documents, and Sheets.

# Product Guide

## 1. Target Users

CrowForge is designed for a diverse audience, including:

*   **Writers and Content Creators:** Individuals who want to leverage AI to assist in their writing process, from drafting to editing.
*   **Everyday Users:** Anyone who wants a secure, local AI assistant to help with various tasks while keeping their data private and avoiding subscription fees.

## 2. Core Features

The application is built around three core pillars, all of which are fully functional:

*   **AI Chat:** A powerful chat interface for interacting with both local and remote AI models, providing a flexible and versatile conversational AI experience.
*   **AI Documents:** A rich-text editor integrated with AI-powered assistance to help users write, edit, and improve their documents.
*   **AI Sheets:** An intelligent spreadsheet application that uses AI to help with data manipulation, analysis, and visualization.

## 3. Design Philosophy

The user interface of CrowForge follows a **minimalist** design philosophy. The focus is on providing a clean, uncluttered, and intuitive user experience that prioritizes content and functionality over ornamentation.

## 4. Monetization Strategy

CrowForge is and will remain a **free and open-source** project. The goal is to provide a powerful AI tool that is accessible to everyone, without the need for a paid license or subscription.
